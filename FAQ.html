Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our catalog, add your desired books to the cart, and proceed to checkout.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and online banking.

3. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website.

4. Can I return a book?

Yes, you can return a book within 30 days of purchase. Please refer to our return policy for more details.

5. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to selected countries. Shipping fees and times may vary.

6. How can I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team via email at support@onlinebookstore.com or call us at 1-800-123-4567.
